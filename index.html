About Us

Fountain of Life Church is a Bible-believing Christian church in Canada, dedicated to worshiping God, teaching His Word, and building a strong community of faith. Our heart is to see lives transformed through the power of the Gospel and the love of Jesus Christ.

We are a welcoming church family where people of all ages and backgrounds can grow spiritually, develop meaningful relationships, and discover their God-given purpose. Through worship services, prayer, teaching, and community outreach, we seek to live out our faith and make a positive impact both within and beyond our church.

At Fountain of Life Church, we believe in faith, love, service, and spiritual growth, and we are committed to creating an environment where everyone can encounter God and flourish in their walk with Him.
